About Me

Hello, my name is Dima. I am from Belarus, but I have been living in Poland for five years. I became interested in programming during the coronavirus pandemic when I had more time and a desire to try something new. I started by creating simple web pages. At first, it was difficult, but I persisted. Once I understood HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I decided to try finding a job. My first company was Imado, which specializes in creating web pages using WordPress. There, I improved my knowledge and understanding of programming. We built websites using ACF (Advanced Custom Fields) and various plugins like Contact Form 7 and Gravity Forms. I also learned how to work with servers and use GitHub. Additionally, I gained experience in testing web pages.

As for my soft skills, I have a passion for learning foreign languages. I speak Russian, Polish, English, and am currently learning German. I also have several years of experience as a tutor of Polish for foreigners. I enjoy communicating with people.
